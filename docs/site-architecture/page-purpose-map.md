# Page Purpose Map — ThunderHouse

Each page has one primary purpose. Every section on the page supports that purpose.

---

## Home Page (`/en`, `/es`)

**Primary purpose:** Orient a new visitor to who ThunderHouse is, what problem it solves, and what to do next.

**Target audience:** Any professional service business owner who landed via search, referral, or social.

**Primary CTA:** Get Your Free AI Audit → `/ai-intake-audit`

**Secondary CTA:** See How It Works → `/process`

**What it must communicate:**
1. Who we help (doctors, attorneys, CPAs)
2. What problem we solve (losing clients in 4 places)
3. How we solve it (AI systems)
4. That we are local and bilingual
5. Why they should trust us (case studies, why us)

**What it must NOT do:** Over-explain every service. Get the visitor to click the audit CTA.

**Connection to other pages:** Every section links out — LeakSystem → audit, Services → service detail, Industries → industry pages, Process → process page, CtaBanner → audit.

---

## AI Intake Audit Page (`/en/ai-intake-audit`, `/es/ai-intake-audit`)

**Primary purpose:** Convert visitors into audit bookings (Calendly).

**Target audience:** Business owner who is considering ThunderHouse or was referred.

**Primary CTA:** Request My Free Audit → Calendly

**SEO target:** "free AI audit for [doctors/attorneys/CPAs]", "AI client acquisition audit"

**What it must communicate:**
1. What the audit is (30-min call + written summary)
2. What gets reviewed (5 specific areas)
3. What they receive (written findings, prioritized recommendations)
4. Who it's for (specific industries)
5. That there is no obligation, no pitch, no commitment
6. FAQ to address skepticism

**What it must NOT do:** Be vague about what happens. Every concern that could stop a booking must be answered.

**Connection:** This is the primary conversion page. Everything links to it.

## Services Overview (`/en/services`, `/es/services`)

**Primary purpose:** Orient visitors to the three service areas and funnel them to the most relevant service detail page.

**Target audience:** Visitor who wants to understand what ThunderHouse builds before booking.

**Primary CTA:** Individual service cards linking to detail pages

**Secondary CTA:** "Not sure? The audit tells you." → `/ai-intake-audit`

**What it must communicate:**
- The three service areas (intake, follow-up, operations)
- A one-line description of each
- That services follow from a diagnosis, not a menu
