# Offer Architecture — ThunderHouse

## Primary Offer: Free AI Client Acquisition Audit

### What it is
A 30-minute video discovery call followed by a written summary of findings delivered within 2 business days.

### What is covered in the audit
1. Intake process review — how leads/patients/clients contact the business and what happens next
2. Follow-up system review — automated vs. manual, response time, drop-off analysis
3. Appointment and scheduling flow — ease of booking, reminder systems, no-show rate
4. Operational workflow — where staff spends manual time, bottlenecks, data re-entry
5. Digital presence spot-check — Google Business Profile, website conversion signals

### What the client receives
- Written summary of findings (specific, not generic)
- Identified gaps ranked by impact
- Prioritized list of AI systems that would address those gaps
- Realistic scope and cost estimates for implementation
- No obligation to proceed

### Why this converts
- Zero financial risk (it's free)
- Specific value even without buying (they keep the diagnosis)
- Positions ThunderHouse as knowledgeable before the sale
- Creates a natural handoff to implementation proposals
- Especially effective for Puerto Rico market where trust precedes transaction

### How to deliver it
1. Prospect books via Calendly (/contact page or /ai-intake-audit page)
2. Pre-call: brief questionnaire or preparation notes sent automatically
3. 30-minute call: discovery focused, not sales-focused
4. Post-call: written summary emailed within 2 business days
5. Follow-up: optional proposal if gaps align with ThunderHouse services

## Service Areas

### 1. Client Intake Automation
**What it is:** Systems that capture, qualify, and route incoming leads automatically.
**Who needs it:** Businesses with high inquiry volume, inconsistent response times, or leads coming from multiple channels.
**Typical deliverables:** Intake form with routing logic, auto-response sequences, scheduling flow integration, document collection trigger
**Implementation time:** 1–3 weeks
**Ongoing:** Monitor, adjust, optimize

### 2. AI Follow-Up Systems
**What it is:** Automated sequences that follow up with leads and remind existing clients.
**Who needs it:** Businesses with no-show problems, cold leads, or manual reminder processes.
**Typical deliverables:** SMS/WhatsApp/email reminder sequences, missed call response automation, re-engagement flows, after-hours AI chat
**Implementation time:** 1–2 weeks
**Ongoing:** Sequence performance monitoring, A/B adjustments

### 3. Operations Automation
**What it is:** Workflow automation that eliminates manual, repetitive internal tasks.
**Who needs it:** Businesses where staff spends significant time on data entry, coordination, or status updates.
**Typical deliverables:** CRM integrations, task handoff automation, document collection workflows, reporting dashboards
**Implementation time:** 2–6 weeks (depends on complexity)
**Ongoing:** System maintenance, integration updates

## Pricing Philosophy

ThunderHouse does NOT publish pricing on the website. Reasons:
1. Each implementation is scoped after the audit — no two are identical
2. Publishing pricing without context invites comparison-shopping without trust
3. The audit creates context that justifies pricing naturally
4. Puerto Rico market responds better to relationship-first pricing discussions

Pricing conversations happen after the audit, when ThunderHouse has demonstrated value and the prospect has received something useful (the written findings) without paying.

---

## What ThunderHouse Does NOT Sell

- Generic website builds (not in scope)
- SEO retainers (not in scope)
- Social media management (not in scope)
- Software licenses (we implement, we don't resell SaaS)
- Data science or BI dashboards (removed from scope in rebuild)
- Custom AI model development (not in scope)

These exclusions keep ThunderHouse focused and credible within its three service areas.

## Upsell Path

```
Free Audit
    ↓
Written Findings
    ↓
Implementation Proposal (one or more service areas)
    ↓
Phase 1 Implementation (typically the highest-impact leak first)
    ↓
Ongoing optimization / Phase 2 (additional service areas)
```

Most clients start with one service area and expand as they see results. The diagnostic model creates natural upsell opportunities because the audit report often identifies gaps in multiple areas.
