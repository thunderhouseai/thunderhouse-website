# Local SEO Plan — ThunderHouse

ThunderHouse serves two markets: Puerto Rico (primary) and Orlando (secondary). Local SEO strategy must address both.

---

## Geographic Targets

| Market | Primary Industries | Priority |
|--------|-------------------|----------|
| Puerto Rico (island-wide) | Medical, Legal, CPA | Primary |
| Orlando, FL | Medical, Legal, CPA | Secondary |
| San Juan, PR | All | High (largest market) |

---

## Google Business Profile

**Setup checklist:**
- [ ] Business name: ThunderHouse
- [ ] Category: Technology Consulting / Business Technology

- [ ] Languages: English + Spanish
- [ ] Business description: Written in both EN and ES
- [ ] Services listed: Client Intake Automation, AI Follow-Up Systems, Operations Automation, AI Audit
- [ ] Phone: Add when confirmed
- [ ] Website: https://thunderhouseai.com
- [ ] Photos: Logo, team, screenshots of work (no mock-ups)

**Posts strategy:**
- Post monthly at minimum
- Share completed client wins (with permission)
- Post in both English and Spanish

## Citation Building

After launch, submit to:

1. **Google Business Profile** (highest priority)
2. **Bing Places for Business**
3. **Apple Maps Connect**
4. **Yelp for Business**
5. **LinkedIn Company Page** (already referenced in footer)
6. **Chamber of Commerce directories:**
   - Puerto Rico Chamber of Commerce
   - Greater Orlando Chamber of Commerce (if applicable)
7. **Industry-specific directories:**
   - Medical: Healthgrades (for partners, not ThunderHouse itself)
   - Legal: Martindale-Hubbell (for partner context)
   - CPA: AICPA directory (as trusted vendor)

**Consistency rule:** Business name, phone, address must be **identical** across all citations. Even small variations (LLC vs. no LLC, abbreviations) hurt local ranking.

## Content for Local SEO

Pages that target local keywords:
- `/en/industries/doctors` → "medical practices Puerto Rico"
- `/en/industries/attorneys` → "law firms Puerto Rico"
- `/en/industries/cpas` → "CPA firms Puerto Rico", "Orlando accountants"
- `/en/ai-intake-audit` → "free AI audit Puerto Rico"

**Spanish pages (`/es/...`) provide native language signals** — Google indexes these separately and they can rank for Spanish-language searches in Puerto Rico.

## Link Building Strategy

**Phase 1 (post-launch):**
- Request backlinks from client websites (if permitted)
- Guest posts on Puerto Rico business blogs
- LinkedIn articles published in both EN and ES

**Phase 2 (3–6 months):**
- Partner with local associations (Colegio de Médicos, Bar Association) for vendor listings
- Pitch PR business media (El Nuevo Día business section, Caribbean Business)
